Android Printing Instructions

To print your badge on Android:

  1. The print dialog should open automatically
  2. If not, tap the three-dot menu (⋮) in your browser
  3. Select "Print" from the menu
  4. Choose your printer or save as PDF
  5. Adjust settings if needed and tap "Print"

Tip: Make sure the USB printer is connected to this server computer.
